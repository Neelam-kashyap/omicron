Omicron Report

Intro : Omicron is new coronavirus variant called B.1.1.529, It has 30 mutations in the spike protein and 10 mutations in the ACE2 receptors.

First reported : Botswana and South Africa

In INDIA : First case was reported in Patna (25-year-old-man). Delhi reported 1,313 fresh cases, 423 recoveries in 24 hours on Thursday. Maharashtra reported 5,368 fresh cases on Thursday.

Is it dangerous : Omicron is milder than Delta variant but its transmissibility (ability to transmitt) is higher than Delta.
